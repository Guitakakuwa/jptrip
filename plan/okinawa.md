# Okinawa — 3 Nights (Sep 20–22)

Pace: 1 dive day, 1 north day (Churaumi + Kouri), 1 beach/return day. Rental car essential.

Resort options in [`accommodation.md`](accommodation.md).

## Food

**Naha**
- **Yunangi** (home cooking — taco rice, goya champuru, Okinawa soba)
- **Ufuya** (traditional house, course menu)
- **Yotsutake** (Ryukyu court cuisine + dance show)
- **Pork Tamago Onigiri Honten** (artisanal Spam musubi, ¥400)
- **Blue Seal Ice Cream** (Okinawan-American hybrid, try beni-imo)

**Onna / mid-island**
- **Hamabe no Chaya** (cliffside cocktails, sunset)
- **Captain Kangaroo** (cult Nago burger spot)
- **Cafe Yabusachi** (cliff café, ocean view lunch)
- **Aqua Mauna Onna** (seafood + awamori)

**Motobu / north**
- **Pizza in the Sky / Yamacha-Sashi** (cliff pizzeria, insane Pacific view)
- **Hana Hana Café** (Bashofu lunch, sweet potato)
- **Toraya** (Okinawa soba in a thatched-roof hut)

**American Village / Chatan**
- **Transit Café** (sunset over Sunset Beach)
- **Captain's Inn** (Okinawan-American steakhouse, since '55)
- **Vongo & Anchor** (open-air craft beer + brunch)

## Tips

- **Rent car at Naha airport** — book in advance, English nav. Driving on Okinawa is essential (transit is bus-only outside Naha).
- **Don Quijote Naha** is the best place to buy a swimsuit (base.md shopping item) before heading to the beach.
- **Reef-safe sunscreen** required at some beaches.
- **No flights for 18 hours after diving** — if you dive on the 22nd morning, you can fly that evening (~5pm) per most operator rules. Verify with your operator.
- Holiday returns (Sep 22 if Sep 23 is a holiday) = expect 30-min line at Naha security. Arrive 2.5 hrs early.
