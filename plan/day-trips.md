Pick one or two during the Tokyo blocks.

|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Yokohama — Experiences (the saved cluster)
- **Yokohama Red Brick Warehouse (Aka-Renga Sōko)** ✓ saved — Meiji-era warehouse turned shops/restaurants on the bay
- **Aka-Renga Park** ✓ saved — open lawn next to the warehouses, sunset spot
- **Yokohama Chinatown** ✓ saved — Japan's biggest, evening lanterns
- **Yokohama Stadium** ✓ saved — BayStars (NPB); Sep regular-season home games likely available

## Yokohama — Food
- **Manchinrō** (Chinatown, xiaolongbao + Cantonese, since 1892)
- **Heichinrou** (Chinese banquet hall, Yokohama institution)
- **Bills Yokohama Akarenga** (waterfront brunch in red-brick warehouse)
- **Motion Blue Yokohama** (jazz dinner club, Akarenga)
- **Cup Noodle Museum food court** (international noodle stalls)
- **鳥貴族 横浜西口店 (Torikizoku Yokohama West)** ✓ saved — every skewer ¥350, cheap group dinner near the station
- **感動の肉と米 青葉台** ✓ saved — Aobadai (Den-en-toshi line) wagyu + rice set bargain; only worth detour from central Yokohama corridor

## Kamakura — Food
- **Hatsuhana Kamakura** (handmade soba on tatami)
- **Magokoro** (vegan + organic in old house)
- **Komachi Street snacks**: matcha soft serve, shirasu (whitebait) toast
- **Yuigahama beach huts** (summer only — closing for the season)

## Kawaguchiko — Food
- **Hoto Fudo** (hoto noodles in iron-pot stew — the regional dish)
- **Lake Kawaguchiko café strip** for Fuji views

---

## Kawasaki — Food (only if passing through)
- **Kanno Coffee Kawasaki Arima** ✓ saved — third-wave roaster, 4.0★ but cult-loved, easy detour from Yokohama corridor
- **Sushiro Kawasaki Mizusawa** ✓ saved — conveyor-belt sushi, only worth it if hungry near the store

## Okutama — Camping / Nature
- **Chōjayashiki Camping Ground** ✓ saved — riverside campsite, 4.0★, deep in Okutama. Needs gear; check Sep typhoon risk
- **Okutama-machi (Nishitama)** ✓ saved — base area for hikes / Hatonosu gorge / Tamagawa source

## Recommendations

- **If bamboo grove matters** (base.md "alternative to Kyoto") → Kamakura over Yokohama
- **If Fuji is a priority** → split Hakone + Kawaguchiko (2-night extension), or Mt Takao for a low-effort glimpse
- **If theme parks** → Disney Sea > Disney Land. But this eats a full day + recovery day. Only if top-3 priority.
