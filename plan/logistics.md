# Logistics

## Transportation

- **IC card:** Welcome Suica at Haneda arrivals (28-day, no deposit). Or add Suica to Apple Wallet before arriving.
- **JR Pass:** **Not worth it** — no Shinkansen in this itinerary, math doesn't break even.
- **Hakone:** Hakone Free Pass ¥6,100 covers everything in the loop + Romancecar discount.
- **Okinawa:** Rental car essential. Book at Naha airport, English nav.
- **Takkyubin:** Use it Hakone → Tokyo Pt 2 hotel. ¥2,000/bag, arrives next day. Yamato counter in most hotels.
- **Taxi after midnight:** Trains stop ~01:00. ¥2,500 Shibuya ↔ Shinjuku at night.

## Packing (mid-Sep)

- **Tokyo:** 24–28°C day, 18–22°C night. Light layers, rain jacket (typhoon season tail), comfy walking shoes (15K+ steps/day).
- **Okinawa:** 28–32°C day, humid. Swimsuit, reef-safe sunscreen, water shoes for Blue Cave, rashguard.
- **Hakone:** A few degrees cooler than Tokyo. Light sweater for evening.
- **Universal:** Portable charger, IC card, JPY cash (¥30K starting), passport (always carry), tax-free shop bags.

## Common mistakes to avoid

- **Don't tip.** Anywhere. Ever.
- **No phone calls** on trains, low voices.
- **Don't eat while walking** (except festival / Nakamise / American Village).
- **Cash + IC card both.** Many places still cash-only. 7-Eleven ATMs accept foreign cards.
- **No diving 18 hr before flight.** Our schedule is safe but verify with operator.
- **Disney Sea Premier Access** — reserve via app at 07:00 morning-of, not at the park.
- **Ghibli Museum** — exactly 1 month out at 10:00 JST sharp, sells out in minutes.
- **Don't queue an hour for sushi** when a 10-min walk lands you at an equally good spot.
- **Hakone ryokan + tattoos** — book a kashikiri (private bath) room to avoid issues.
- **Trash bins are rare** — carry trash in a pocket until a konbini.
