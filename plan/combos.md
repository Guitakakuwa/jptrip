# Sample Day Combos

Each combo groups items that fit together geographically and energy-wise.

**Not prescriptive** — just templates. Swap pieces freely.

---

## Combo A — "Welcome Soft Landing" (jetlag day)
**Effort: 2/5 · Exhaustion: Low · Walking: ~3 km**

- Shibuya Scramble (from Mag's Park roof — free)
- Don Quijote Shibuya browse
- Izakaya dinner (Tsubohachi or Uoshin)
- Early sleep

*Best for arrival day or any post-heavy recovery.*

---

## Combo B — "Modern Tokyo Loop"
**Effort: 3/5 · Exhaustion: Med · Walking: ~6 km**

- Meiji Jingu (morning, shrine vibe)
- Takeshita St → Omotesando → Cat Street (walking corridor)
- Nintendo + Pokémon Center Shibuya (Parco)
- Coffee at Koffee Mameya Kakeru
- Shibuya Sky sunset
- Dinner: Uobei or Kaikaya

*Best Day 2 — perfect intro after jetlag.*

---

## Combo C — "Old Tokyo + Sumo"
**Effort: 4/5 · Exhaustion: High · Walking: ~7 km**

- Sensō-ji + Nakamise (morning, before crowds)
- Imado Shrine (cat shrine, 10 min walk)
- Kappabashi Kitchen St for chopsticks + knives
- Walk Sumida River → Skytree
- Sumida Aquarium (otters, jellyfish)
- Train to Ryōgoku (1 stop)
- **SUMO** afternoon at Kokugikan
- Chanko Tomoegata dinner

*Heavy day but everything connects. Only works during Aki Basho (Sep 13–25).*

---

## Combo D — "Shinjuku Day → Night"
**Effort: 4/5 · Exhaustion: High · Walking: ~7 km**

- Shinjuku Gyoen (morning calm)
- Lunch at Tsunahachi or Menya Musashi
- Isetan depachika + Bicqlo shopping
- Godzilla Head photo
- Bar Benfiddich (17:30 open)
- Omoide Yokocho yakitori crawl
- 0 Byou Lemon Sour
- Samurai Restaurant
- Golden Gai bar hop

*The big Tokyo night. Plan a rest day after.*

---

## Combo E — "Yokohama Day Trip"
**Effort: 3/5 · Exhaustion: Med · Walking: ~5 km**

- Cup Noodle Museum (book slot)
- Chinatown lunch
- Trick Art Cruise or Museum
- Yamashita Park → Osanbashi Pier walk
- Cosmo Clock 21 Ferris wheel at sunset
- Akarenga (Red Brick Warehouse) dinner

*Half- to full-day. 35-min train back.*

---

## Combo F — "Kamakura Alternative" (instead of Yokohama)
**Effort: 4/5 · Exhaustion: Med-High · Walking: ~8 km**

- Great Buddha (Kotokuin)
- Hokokuji Bamboo Grove + matcha (Kyoto alternative ✓ base.md)
- Komachi Street snacks
- Hase-dera temple + sea view
- Optional: Enoshima sunset

*Beats Yokohama if bamboo + temples matter more than novelty.*

---

## Combo G — "TeamLab + Tsukiji + Ginza"
**Effort: 3/5 · Exhaustion: Med · Walking: ~5 km**

- Tsukiji Outer Market breakfast crawl (09:00–11:00)
- Yurikamome to Toyosu → TeamLab Planets (book 12:00–13:00 slot)
- Train to Ginza
- Ginza 6 depachika + Itoya
- Custom watch (Knot Ginza)
- Bar High Five
- Wagyu dinner (Ginza Steak or Yoroniku if booked)

*Heavy-eating, low-walking day.*

---

## Combo H — "Recovery + Round 1"
**Effort: 3/5 · Exhaustion: Med · Walking: ~4 km**

- Slow café morning (Bear Pond or Fuglen)
- Head spa appointment
- Chinzanso afternoon tea
- Round 1 Stadium Spo-Cha evening (Odaiba)
- Konbini dinner challenge

*Perfect day after Okinawa return, or post-heavy night.*

## Combo I — "Hidden Gems Hop"
**Effort: 3/5 · Exhaustion: Low-Med · Walking: ~6 km**

- Gōtokuji cat temple (morning)
- Shimokitazawa vintage + cafés
- Daikanyama T-Site / Tsutaya
- Nakameguro river walk
- Onibus Coffee
- Toriki Meguro dinner (reserve)

*The locals' Tokyo, low-stress.*

---

## Combo J — "Last Day Easy"
**Effort: 2/5 · Exhaustion: Low · Walking: ~4 km**

- Rikugien Garden morning
- Lunch wherever
- Last shopping pass (Don Quijote or Kappabashi)
- Spa LaQua urban onsen
- Farewell sushi omakase
- One last Shibuya Crossing walk

*Save energy for the morning flight.*

---

## Combo K — "Theme Park All-In" (Disney Sea)
**Effort: 5/5 · Exhaustion: Very High · Walking: 15+ km**

- Disney Sea 08:00 → 22:00
- Tower of Terror, Soaring, Journey to Center of the Earth (priorities)
- Premier Access for 2–3 attractions
- Park dinner

*Only if Disney Sea is a top-3 priority. Eats a full day + recovery.*
