# Hakone — 1 Night Ryokan (Sep 18)

Loop: Romancecar from Shinjuku → Hakone-Yumoto → switchback train → Gora → cable car + ropeway → Owakudani → Lake Ashi → pirate ship → Moto-Hakone → Hakone Shrine → bus back.

Ryokan options in [`accommodation.md`](accommodation.md).

## Experiences

| Experience | Effort | Exhaustion | Time | Reserve? | Why |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Hakone Open-Air Museum** | 3 | Med | 90 min | No | Picasso pavilion + outdoor sculpture — the rare must-do museum |
| **Owakudani sulfur vents + black eggs** | 2 | Low | 60 min | No | Volcanic crater, ropeway over steam, Fuji views ✓ base.md "if time" |
| **Hakone Pirate Ship across Lake Ashi** | 2 | Low | 60 min | Hakone Pass | Lake cruise, view of Hakone Shrine torii from water |
| **Hakone Shrine + Lake torii** | 2 | Low | 60 min | No | The vermillion-torii-in-the-lake icon — queue early |
| **Pola Museum of Art** | 2 | Low | 2 hrs | No | Impressionists in cedar forest — exceptional rainy-day swap |
| **Hakone Venetian Glass Museum** | 2 | Low | 90 min | No | Murano glass in a Gora villa, rainy alt |
| **Onsen at ryokan** | 1 | Low | 60 min | Included | The whole point ✓ base.md |
| **Kaiseki dinner at ryokan** | 1 | Low | 2 hrs | Included | 10+ courses, seasonal |
| **Bakery & Table Hakone (lake foot-bath)** | 1 | Low | 60 min | No | Eat lakeside with foot in onsen water |
| **Hakone Free Pass** | — | — | — | Yes | ¥6,100, covers all loop transport + Romancecar discount |

## Food beyond ryokan

- **Hatsuhana** (Yumoto soba, walk from station)
- **Bakery & Table Hakone** (lakeside lunch)
- **Marutomi Honten** (eel rice bowls, Yumoto)
- **Naraya Café** (free foot-bath while you sip coffee, Miyanoshita)

## Tips

- Reserve a ryokan with **kashikiri (private bath)** — solves tattoos.
- Owakudani occasionally closes for volcanic activity — check morning of.
- Send big bags via **takkyubin** from Tokyo Pt 1 hotel directly to Tokyo Pt 2 hotel. Bring overnight bag only.
- Romancecar reserved seats — book on Odakyu site or at station.
