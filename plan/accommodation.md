# Accommodation

The couple needs their own place. One guest stays with Gui.

Recommendation: **Shibuya area** for both Tokyo blocks — central, walkable, best transit + nightlife.

---

## Couple's Tokyo hotel — mid-upper (¥25–45K/night)

| Hotel | Vibe | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Trunk Hotel Yoyogi Park** | Design boutique | Walkable to Harajuku + Shibuya |
| **Hotel Indigo Shibuya** | New, rooftop bar | Best Shibuya location |
| **Sequence Miyashita Park** | Modern, mid-budget | On top of Miyashita Park, sub-¥30K |
| **Cerulean Tower Tokyu** | Corporate, big rooms | Unbeatable Shibuya Station access |
| **Hotel Gracery Shinjuku** | Godzilla on the roof | If Shinjuku base preferred |

## Hakone Ryokan (Sep 18) — with private bath

| Ryokan | Why |
|---|---|
| **Gora Hanaougi** | All rooms have private rotenburo, kaiseki, central Gora |
| **Hakone Ginyu** | Cliffside, valley views, modern-traditional |
| **Hoshino KAI Hakone** | Riverside, design-forward, easier English |
| **Hakone Yutowa** | Mid-budget option, still has private baths |

## Okinawa Resort (Sep 20–22)

| Resort | Area | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Halekulani Okinawa** | Onna (mid-island) | Best beach + closest to Blue Cave dive |
| **Hilton Okinawa Sesoko Resort** | Motobu (north) | Closest to Churaumi Aquarium |
| **Hyatt Regency Seragaki Island** | Onna | Private-island feel, mid-priced |
