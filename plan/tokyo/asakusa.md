# Tokyo — Asakusa / Skytree / Ryōgoku

Best 8–10am before tour buses. Naturally pairs with Sumo afternoon at Ryōgoku (one stop on Toei Asakusa Line).

## Experiences

| Experience | Effort | Exhaustion | Time | Reserve? | Why |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Sensō-ji + Kaminarimon Gate + Nakamise-dori** | 2 | Low | 90 min | No | The classic. Go 8–9am. Omikuji fortunes ✓ saved |
| **Imado Shrine (cat shrine)** | 2 | Low | 30 min | No | Origin of maneki-neko, 10 min walk from Sensō-ji — base.md "cat temple" |
| **Kappabashi Kitchen Street** | 2 | Low | 60 min | No | Knives, plastic food samples, chopsticks ✓ base.md |
| **Sumida Park river walk** | 2 | Low | 30 min | No | Skytree views, free |
| **Tokyo Skytree obs deck** | 2 | Low | 90 min | Recommended | Tallest in Japan, but Shibuya Sky is nicer ✓ saved |
| **Sumida Aquarium (Skytree Town 5F)** | 1 | Low | 60 min | No | Ethical otter exhibit ✓ (replaces unethical café), jellyfish room |
| **Tokyo Solamachi mall** | 1 | Low | 60 min | No | At Skytree base, Ghibli store, Pokémon Center |
| **Sumo Aki Basho — Ryōgoku Kokugikan** | 2 | Med | 3–4 hrs | **YES** | Sep 13–27 only window of the year ✓ base.md |
| **Sumo Museum (Kokugikan)** | 1 | Low | 30 min | No | Free, inside the stadium |
| **Yakatabune dinner cruise (Sumida)** | 1 | Low | 2.5 hrs | Yes | Traditional + modern, ¥10K/person |
| **Chopstick making — Hyozaemon Asakusa** | 1 | Low | 45 min | Yes | Polish + carve your own ✓ base.md |
| **Kintsugi workshop** | 2 | Low | 2 hrs | Yes | Asakusa + Hatchobori studios ✓ base.md |

## Food

**Breakfast**
- **Pelican Café** (cult toast specialist, lines)
- **Onigiri Bongo Otsuka** (premium rice balls, detour worth it)
- **Kagetsudo** (giant melonpan at Sensō-ji, eat on the walk)
- **Roast Design Coffee** ✓ saved (Asakusa specialty café, 4.4★ — pour-over + design pastries)

**Lunch**
- **Asakusa Sometaro** (DIY okonomiyaki on tatami)
- **Daikokuya tempura** (Asakusa institution, ebi tendon)
- **Otafuku** (oden, since 1916)
- **Bunsendo** (handmade soba in a 100-year house)
- **Yoshi Soba** (Buddhist-style soba)

**Dinner (post-Sumo at Ryōgoku)**
- **Chanko Tomoegata** (sumo stew, the institution — reserve)
- **Chanko Doji** (cheaper chanko, walk-in OK)
- **Sushi Kappo Tojikura** (Ryōgoku sushi, well-priced)
- **Popeye Beer Pub** (Ryōgoku, 70 craft taps — best beer bar in Tokyo)

**Dessert**
- **Asakusa Silk Pudding**
- **Suzukien Asakusa** (world's strongest matcha gelato, 7 levels)
- **Funawa** (sweet potato yokan + imo yokan parfait)

**Late-night**
- **Hoppy Street** (Asakusa, locals' all-night izakaya alley)
