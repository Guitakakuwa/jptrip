# Tokyo — Hidden Gems Neighborhoods

Setagaya / Daikanyama / Nakameguro / Shimokitazawa / Yanaka / Kagurazaka / Sangenjaya.

Low-effort, café-strolling, vintage shopping. Best half-day combo. The locals' Tokyo.

## Experiences

| Experience | Effort | Exhaustion | Time | Reserve? | Why |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Gōtokuji ("cat temple")** | 2 | Low | 60 min | No | Hundreds of maneki-neko statues — the photographic one ✓ saved |
| **Shimokitazawa vintage crawl** | 3 | Med | 3 hrs | No | Indie clothes, vinyl, cafés, theaters |
| **Daikanyama T-Site (Tsutaya bookstore)** | 1 | Low | 60 min | No | Architectural pilgrimage, world-class bookstore |
| **Nakameguro river walk** | 1 | Low | 60 min | No | Cherry canal even off-season, café row |
| **Yanaka Ginza shitamachi street** | 2 | Low | 90 min | No | Old Tokyo, cats everywhere, sunset stairs |
| **Kagurazaka (former geisha district)** | 2 | Low | 90 min | No | Bistros + small shrines, quiet sophistication |
| **Sangenjaya (Bear Pond + Carrot Tower view)** | 1 | Low | 60 min | No | Free Carrot Tower observation deck |
| **Nezu Museum + garden (Aoyama)** | 2 | Low | 90 min | No | Asian art + secret garden — exceptional |

## Food

- **Tonki Meguro** (tonkatsu institution, no reservations, theater of cooking)
- **Toriki Meguro** (yakitori, Michelin Bib — reserve)
- **Patisserie Paris S'éveille (Jiyugaoka)** — top patissier
- **Onibus Coffee Nakameguro** (specialty roaster, train-track café)
- **Sidewalk Stand Nakameguro** (toast + coffee, locals only)
- **Fuglen Tomigaya** (Norwegian-Japanese third wave)
- **Camelback Tomigaya** (tamago sando legend)
- **Bear Pond Espresso Sangenjaya** (only 30 espressos/day before noon)
