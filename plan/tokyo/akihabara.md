# Tokyo — Akihabara / Kanda

Best afternoons. Density of weird is the point.

## Experiences

| Experience | Effort | Exhaustion | Time | Reserve? | Why |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Yodobashi-Akiba mega-electronics** | 3 | Med | 2 hrs | No | Suitcases ✓, cameras, kitchen ✓ — base.md shopping |
| **Super Potato retro game shop** (Retrokan Ota Road) | 2 | Low | 60 min | No | 3 floors of pre-2000 games ✓ saved |
| **Mandarake Akihabara Complex** | 2 | Med | 90 min | No | Vintage manga + figures + cels |
| **Don Quijote Akihabara** | 2 | Med | 60 min | No | Multi-floor, AKB48 theater on top |
| **GIGO Akihabara** | 2 | Med | 60 min | No | Arcade, claw machines, music games |
| **Maid café (@home cafe)** | 2 | Med | 60 min | No | Touristy but iconic; @home is the cleanest version |
| **Kanda Myōjin shrine** | 2 | Low | 45 min | No | Tech-blessing shrine — locals bring laptops |
| **Custom watch — Knot Marunouchi** | 1 | Low | 60 min | Recommended | Pick case + strap, 30-min assembly — base.md ✓ |

## Food

**Lunch**
- **Kanda Matsuya** (soba since 1884)
- **Hinoya Curry Akihabara** (wagyu curry, cheap, beloved)
- **Mutekiya Ikebukuro-style ramen (Akiba branch)**
- **Niboshi Ramen Nagi Akiba** (anchovy ramen)

**Dinner**
- **Yakiniku Like Akihabara** (solo-style yakiniku, surprisingly great)
- **Toriyoshi** (Kanda yakitori counter)
- **Kanda Yabu Soba** (handmade soba institution)

**Themed café (one-and-done)**
- **Pokémon Café Nihombashi** (10 min from Akiba — reserve exactly 31 days out at 18:00 JST)
- **Capcom Café** (Shinjuku branch better)
- **Final Fantasy Eorzea Café** (Akiba)
