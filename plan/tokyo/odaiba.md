# Tokyo — Odaiba / Tokyo Bay

Half-day, lots under one roof. Best in evening for skyline.

## Experiences

| Experience | Effort | Exhaustion | Time | Reserve? | Why |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Round 1 Stadium DiverCity (Spo-Cha)** | 3 | High | 2–3 hrs | No | Sports + arcade + go-karts + karaoke under one roof ✓ base.md must-do ✓ saved |
| **Joypolis indoor theme park** | 3 | High | 3 hrs | No | Sega-themed, all-indoor coasters + VR — Mario Kart substitute |
| **Unicorn Gundam DiverCity** | 1 | Low | 30 min | No | Free, life-size statue, animated at sunset |
| **Rainbow Bridge night walk** | 2 | Med | 60 min | No | Pedestrian path with skyline view |
| **Oedo Onsen Monogatari** | 2 | Low | 3 hrs | No | Theme-park onsen — verify open status, has relocated |
