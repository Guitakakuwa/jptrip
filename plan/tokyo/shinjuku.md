# Tokyo — Shinjuku

Daytime: gardens + shopping. Evening: neon, yakitori, bars. The big night-out neighborhood.

## Experiences

| Experience | Effort | Exhaustion | Time | Reserve? | Why |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Shinjuku Gyoen garden** | 2 | Low | 90 min | No | Best garden in central Tokyo — Japanese + French + English ✓ saved |
| **Tokyo Metropolitan Gov't Bldg obs deck** | 1 | Low | 45 min | No | Free top-floor view, Fuji on clear days |
| **Isetan Shinjuku depachika** | 2 | Med | 60 min | No | The world's most beautiful basement |
| **Bicqlo (Bic Camera + Uniqlo)** | 2 | Med | 90 min | No | Skincare, suitcases, clothes — 1 stop for half the list |
| **Godzilla Head photo (Hotel Gracery)** | 1 | Low | 15 min | No | Kabukicho rooftop, free — base.md item |
| **Omoide Yokocho yakitori alleys** | 2 | Med | 60 min | No | Tiny stalls, smoke, beer |
| **Golden Gai bar hop** | 2 | High | 2–3 hrs | No (look for "tourist welcome" signs) | 200 tiny bars, 6 seats each |
| **Samurai Restaurant show** | 2 | Med | 90 min | Yes | Robot Restaurant successor — base.md |
| **0 Byou Lemon Sour Sakaba** | 1 | Low | 45 min | No | The viral "zero-second lemon" — base.md |
| **Kabukicho neon walk** | 1 | Low | 30 min | No | Best after 21:00 |
| **Bar Benfiddich** | 1 | Low | 60 min | Recommended | Legendary herbalist cocktails, opens 17:30 |
| **Tokyo Daijingu shrine** | 2 | Low | 30 min | No | Hidden love shrine, very local |

## Food

**Breakfast**
- **Blue Bottle Shinjuku (NEWoMan)**
- **Citron Aoyama** (matcha + tartine)
- **Hoshino Coffee** (fluffy soufflé pancakes)

**Lunch**
- **Tsunahachi tempura** (since 1923, ¥2,500 set)
- **Menya Musashi** (rich tonkotsu ramen)
- **Fūunji Shinjuku** ✓ saved (Tokyo's most famous tsukemen — dipping ramen, queue early)
- **ICHIRAN Shinjuku Kabukicho** ✓ saved (solo-booth tonkotsu, 24h, easy English ordering)
- **Kushiyaki Bistro Fukumimi** (yakitori lunch sets)
- **Funabashiya Shinjuku** (tempura + soba combo)
- **Berg** (60-year coffee + sausage bar, Shinjuku Station East)

**Dinner**
- **Omoide Yokocho crawl** — Asadachi (offal), Kabuto (eel), any yakitori stall
- **Tsunahachi** (also dinner)
- **Tempura Funabashiya** (upscale tempura)
- **Uoshin Shinjuku** (huge izakaya, sashimi rocks)
- **Kappo Yoshiba** (sumo theme + chankonabe, less touristy)
- **Torikizoku Shinjuku** (every skewer ¥350, cheap group fun)
- **CoCo Ichibanya** (curry, perfect drunk food)

**Café / Dessert**
- **Pierre Hermé Aoyama** (pastry pilgrimage, walkable from Shinjuku-sanchome)
- **Kagari Shinjuku** (premium dorayaki + matcha)

**Late-night**
- **CoCo Ichibanya** or **Ichiran Shinjuku** (open very late)
- **Konbini** in Kabukicho — basically a 24-hr food court
- **Bar Trench Ebisu** (cocktails till 03:00 if night extends)
