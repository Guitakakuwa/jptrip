# Tokyo — Ginza / Tsukiji / Toyosu

Mornings: Tsukiji. Late afternoon: Ginza shopping. Evening: Toyosu TeamLab or Ginza dining.

## Experiences

| Experience | Effort | Exhaustion | Time | Reserve? | Why |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Tsukiji Outer Market food crawl** | 3 | Med | 2 hrs | No | Best food walking in Tokyo — get there 09:00 |
| **TeamLab Planets Toyosu (DMM)** | 2 | Med | 90 min | **YES** | Water-floor immersive art ✓ base.md must-do ✓ saved |
| **Toyosu Senkyaku Banrai market** | 2 | Med | 90 min | No | Replaced Tsukiji inner market, sushi breakfast |
| **Ginza Six + Itoya stationery** | 2 | Med | 2 hrs | No | Rooftop garden, depachika, 12 floors of stationery |
| **Custom watch — Knot Ginza / Seiko Boutique** | 1 | Low | 60 min | Recommended | Base.md ✓ |
| **Bar High Five (Ginza)** | 1 | Low | 90 min | Recommended | Hidden, top-5 Tokyo cocktail bar |
| **Tokyo Station Marunouchi night façade** | 1 | Low | 30 min | No | Brick architecture lit up |

## Food

**Breakfast (Tsukiji)**
- **Tsukiji Itadori Bekkan** (uni rice bowl on the street)
- **Yamacho** (tamagoyaki on a stick, ¥150)
- **Sushizanmai Tsukiji main** (24-hr institution)
- **Marutoyo Suisan** (tuna sashimi platter ¥1,000)
- **Tsukiji Sushi Sei** (mid-range counter, English menu)

**Lunch (Ginza)**
- **Birdland** (Michelin yakitori lunch ¥3,800 — best-value Michelin in Tokyo)
- **Tempura Kondo** (Michelin 2★ tempura lunch ¥8,000)
- **Yakitori Tori Shige** (Ginza basement counter)
- **Kyubey Ginza** (legendary sushi, lunch sets from ¥6K)
- **Manten Sushi Marunouchi** (approachable omakase ¥6–8K)

**Dinner (Ginza)**
- **Sushi Tokami** (high omakase ¥30K — reserve 60 days)
- **Sushi Ichi Ginza** (approachable omakase ¥15–20K)
- **Ginza Steak** (all-you-can-eat A5 wagyu in 1 hr, ¥15K — wild group experience)
- **Hibiki Marunouchi** (izakaya in the tower, river view)
- **Ginza Sembikiya Fruit Parlor** (fruit sandwiches + parfaits — dessert dinner)

**Café**
- **Cafe de l'Ambre** (since 1948, aged coffee — time machine)
- **Henri Charpentier Ginza Maison** (savarin crêpes + tea)
- **Tsuruya Yoshinobu** (wagashi made tableside — counter seat)
