# Tokyo — Shibuya / Harajuku / Omotesando

Best mornings → afternoons. Heavy crowds Sat/Sun. All items below within walking distance.

## Experiences

| Experience | Effort | Exhaustion | Time | Reserve? | Why |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Shibuya Scramble Crossing** | 1 | Low | 30 min | No | Iconic. Free overhead view from Mag's Park rooftop ✓ saved |
| **Hachiko Square** | 1 | Low | 15 min | No | Statue + meet-up landmark right at the crossing ✓ saved |
| **Shibuya Sky** sunset | 2 | Low | 90 min | Yes (day-of OK) | Best 360° view, book sunset slot ✓ saved |
| **Meiji Jingu shrine** | 2 | Low | 60 min | No | Forest shrine, calm, central — the essential one ✓ saved |
| **Yoyogi Park** Sunday | 1 | Low | 60 min | No | Rockabilly dancers, buskers, human zoo |
| **Takeshita Street Harajuku** | 2 | Med | 45 min | No | Sensory overload, crêpes, gachapon |
| **Omotesando + Cat Street** | 2 | Low | 90 min | No | Tokyo's "Champs-Élysées," Tadao Ando architecture |
| **Shibuya Parco** (Nintendo / Pokémon / Jump / Capcom on 6F) | 2 | Med | 2 hrs | No | The fandom mall, one elevator ride does the whole 6F ✓ saved |
| **Nintendo Tokyo (Parco 6F)** | 1 | Low | 45 min | No | Splatoon photo op, exclusive merch |
| **Pokémon Center Shibuya (Parco 6F)** | 1 | Low | 30 min | No | Same floor as Nintendo |
| **Jump Store + Capcom Store (Parco)** | 1 | Low | 30 min | No | Same floor — one stop, three shrines |
| **Hands Shibuya** (8 floors) | 2 | Med | 60–90 min | No | Stationery, kitchen, travel, beauty — base.md shopping in one stop ✓ saved |
| **Loft Shibuya** | 2 | Med | 60 min | No | Lifestyle goods, stationery, design — sibling to Hands ✓ saved |
| **Tower Records Shibuya** (9 floors) | 1 | Low | 45 min | No | "No music, no life" — J-pop CDs, vinyl, café ✓ saved |
| **GIGO Shibuya arcade** | 2 | Med | 60 min | No | Purikura, claw machines, retro arcade |
| **Don Quijote Shibuya / Mega Don Quijote** | 2 | Med | 60–90 min | No | Open 24h. Souvenirs, snacks, skincare, weird stuff ✓ saved |
| **Sakura-yu sento (public bath)** | 2 | Low | 90 min | No | ¥520, the locals' move |

## Food

**Breakfast**
- **% Arabica Daikanyama** (matcha latte + croissants)
- **Sarutahiko Coffee Ebisu** (Tokyo's best-balanced specialty roaster)
- **Lattest Omotesando** (latte specialists)
- **Bills Omotesando** (ricotta hotcakes, Australian-Japanese)

**Lunch**
- **Afuri Harajuku** (yuzu shio ramen — bright, light)
- **Maisen Aoyama** (tonkatsu institution)
- **Katsukura Shibuya** (panko-crusted katsu, grind your own sesame)
- **Katsudon-ya Zuicho** ✓ saved (Shibuya katsudon specialist, 4.3★)
- **Kaikaya by the Sea** (seafood izakaya, lunch sets)
- **Uobei Shibuya** (touchscreen conveyor sushi, ¥110/plate, group fun)
- **Sushiro Shibuya Station Square** ✓ saved (¥110/plate conveyor chain, easy group hit)
- **Yakiniku Like Shibuya Udagawacho** ✓ saved (solo-style yakiniku, fast + cheap)
- **Rāmen Nagi BUTAO Shibuya** ✓ saved (niboshi-style ramen, sister to the Golden Gai original)
- **CoCo Ichibanya Shibuya Namikibashi** ✓ saved (curry chain — perfect easy meal, also in Shinjuku)

**Dinner**
- **Han no Daidokoro Honten** (yakiniku, English menu)
- **Kobe Beef Yakiniku YAZAWA Shibuya** ✓ saved (4.8★, premium A5 yakiniku — reserve)
- **Kobe Beef Ramen YAZAWA** ✓ saved (sister ramen-ya, A5 broth)
- **Sushi no Midori Shibuya** (reasonable, worth the queue)
- **Tsubohachi Shibuya** (classic izakaya chain — sashimi + yakitori + beer)
- **Gyukatsu Motomura Shibuya** (sear-your-own beef cutlet on a stone)
- **Ouca** (gourmet ice cream after — try beni-imo)

**Café**
- **Koffee Mameya Kakeru** (Omotesando, single-origin tasting flight, sit-down)
- **Streamer Coffee Shibuya** (latte art champion)
- **Camelback Sandwiches & Espresso** (Tomigaya — tamago sando legend)
- **L'Occitane Café Shibuya** (overlooks Scramble)

**Late-night**
- **Uobei** (open until 3am)
- **Don Quijote Shibuya** snack haul → eat back at hotel
- **Konbini** trifecta (7-Eleven + FamilyMart + Lawson)
